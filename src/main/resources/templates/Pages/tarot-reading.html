My Reading Approach

1

Setting Intention

We begin by creating a sacred space and setting a clear intention for the reading. This focuses the energy and ensures the messages received are aligned with your highest good.

2

Card Selection

Using your energy and the question at hand, I'll guide you through selecting cards or select them on your behalf. Each card position has specific meaning related to your inquiry.

3

Interpretation

I'll interpret the cards' symbolism, position, and relationship to each other, revealing insights about your situation. This combines traditional tarot wisdom with intuitive messages.

4

Reflection & Discussion

We'll discuss how the reading applies to your specific situation, exploring possible meanings and applications. This is an interactive process where your input enhances the reading's depth.

5

Practical Guidance

Every reading concludes with practical insights and action steps you can take based on the wisdom revealed. This transforms divination into meaningful change in your life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to have a specific question for a reading?

While specific questions can help focus a reading, they're not required. General readings about your current life path or energy can be just as insightful. If you're unsure, we can work together to clarify what information would be most helpful for you.

How accurate are tarot readings?

Tarot readings offer insights into current energies, potential outcomes, and hidden influences. They're most accurate when viewed as guidance rather than fixed predictions. Remember that you have free will to shape your future, and the cards illuminate possibilities rather than certainties.

How should I prepare for my reading?

Take some time for quiet reflection before your session. Consider what you most want clarity about. Come with an open mind and heart. For remote readings, find a private, comfortable space where you won't be interrupted. Avoid alcohol or substances before your reading.

What if I get "negative" cards?

There are no inherently "bad" cards in the tarot. Each card offers valuable lessons and insights. Cards that appear challenging often represent important growth opportunities or necessary awareness. I focus on empowering interpretations that help you navigate all life situations with wisdom.

How often should I get a tarot reading?

This varies by individual and situation. For ongoing guidance, once every 3-6 months provides a good balance. For specific situations, a reading at the beginning and perhaps a follow-up can be helpful. I discourage becoming dependent on readings for daily decisions.

Do you offer remote/online readings?

Yes, I provide readings via video call (Zoom, FaceTime, etc.) or phone. Remote readings can be just as powerful as in-person sessions. For video calls, I position the camera so you can see the cards as they're drawn and interpreted.
