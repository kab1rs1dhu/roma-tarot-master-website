Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Karma?

Karma is the spiritual principle of cause and effect, where your intentions and actions create corresponding energy that returns to you. It's not punishment or reward, but rather a natural law of energy balance that spans across lifetimes.

How do I know if I need Karma Healing?

You might benefit from Karma Healing if you experience recurring patterns in relationships, persistent financial struggles despite your efforts, unexplained health issues, or a sense of being "stuck" in certain areas of your life despite trying various solutions.

What can I expect to feel after a session?

Many clients report feeling lighter, more peaceful, and having greater clarity. Some experience profound emotional releases during or after the session. Physical sensations like tingling, warmth, or a release of tension are common. Effects can continue to unfold over the following weeks.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies depending on your individual situation. Some clients experience significant shifts after just one session, while others with deeper or more complex patterns may benefit from a series of 3-5 sessions. We'll discuss your specific needs during your initial assessment.

Is Karma Healing the same as past life regression?

While both may address past lives, they are different approaches. Past life regression uses hypnosis to access specific memories, while Karma Healing focuses on identifying and clearing the energetic imprints from past experiences without necessarily needing to recall them in detail.

Can Karma Healing be done remotely?

Yes, Karma Healing can be effectively conducted through distance sessions. Energy work does not require physical proximity to be powerful and transformative. Many clients prefer the comfort of receiving the healing in their own space.
