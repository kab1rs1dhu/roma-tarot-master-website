Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to believe in angels for Angel Healing to work?

While an open mind enhances the experience, you don't need a specific belief system. Angels respond to invitation and respect free will. Many skeptical clients have been surprised by the tangible sensations and accurate messages they receive during sessions.

What might I feel during an Angel Healing session?

Physical sensations vary widely, but may include warmth, tingling, gentle pressure, emotional release, deep peace, or subtle energy movements. Some people see colors or light, while others receive intuitive insights or memories. Each experience is unique.

How is Angel Healing different from Reiki?

While both work with universal life energy, Angel Healing specifically channels the energy and guidance of angelic beings. Reiki typically follows a specific system of hand positions, while Angel Healing is more intuitive and may include direct messages from the angelic realm.

Can I request to work with specific angels?

Absolutely! If you feel drawn to work with particular archangels or angels, we can set that intention at the beginning of your session. However, sometimes different angels than expected may come forward if their energies better match your current needs.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies by individual and intention. Some experience significant shifts after a single session, while others benefit from regular sessions as part of their spiritual practice. We can discuss what might be best for your specific situation after your initial experience.

Can Angel Healing be done remotely?

Yes, Angel Healing is very effective in distance sessions. Angels exist beyond physical limitations of time and space, so they can work with your energy field regardless of physical location. Remote sessions are conducted via phone or video call.
